About Me

I am an entry-level SOC Analyst with hands-on experience in SIEM monitoring, log analysis, and threat detection. Based in Bangalore, India, I specialize in identifying suspicious activities, analyzing authentication logs, and investigating potential security incidents.

My expertise spans the full SOC workflow — from initial alert triage and log correlation through to structured incident investigation.
